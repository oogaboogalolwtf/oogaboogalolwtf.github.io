01 ABOUT

What even is Brainiacs.in

You know what? That is a good question and the answer is really funny. It is because the ideator keeps loves to make new friends and he would ask them which anime/fictional character he resembles the most but doing this over and over again got repetitive and hard to recall whom gave you which role and such. You can imagine that. Sooo yeahhh! That was the main motivation behind this project. To centralize that lol.

"I named it Brainiacs.in 'cuz I had a free domain."

There’s no other deep reasoning behind the name honestly.

LaV Creator of Brainiacs.in

02 HOW IT WORKS

Steps to make a squad

Make your own unique crew/squad in 3 steps. It can’t more simpler than that.

  • Name Your Crew

    Pick a cool and unique name for your crew. Make sure it's memorable.

  • Match Your Friends

    Think about each friend’s personality & quirks. Who do they remind you of in the fiction world?

  • Share With The World

    Show off your crew with the link that’s generated.
