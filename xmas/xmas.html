this page is only for

It's the 24th of December,
the brighest eve as we say.
But let's rewind 4 months ago,
we hadn't even met that day.
The evening of 24th August
was the brightest day of my life,
Cuz that's the day, when you had arrive.

You are the reason my monochrome life,
has been painted, all in vibrance.
That's the angelic aura radiated by you.
Hadn't it been for you,
this man wouldn't have rizz,
And this poem wouldn't exist.

The way I texted you first was a mistake
And if I were you, I would have blocked me
Because I started off so creepily.
But now that we've been stuck for so long,
I guess you've already realized that I was your cute mistake
And you were my beautiful one 😉

Fast-forward to today, just two days before x-max
I'm writing this poem under the cold moonlight
With the thoughts of a girl with 5 fishes,
Or maybe her pleasant kisses.
And I hope you're doing well too, smiling as you read
But you may need to watch out for Santa!
Cuz he may abduct you,
For all, I wrote your name for christmas gift request.
